ABOUT ME

Ola is an English Premier League enthusiast. He likes good football, in English terms, or what is called soccer in the US.

He follows the top 6 teams avidly. While mostly interested in teams that have historically been successfull, he finds the character of some teams most appealing

Top on that list is Manchester United (ManU). ManU has not performed well in recent times. Their results over the years are however unequivocal. Ola had rooted for ManU on many occasions

Next on the list is Liverpool. Liverpool resurgence to top flights in recent is remarkable. Ola rooted for Liverpool against any team in the European cup of nations

Then there is Arsenal and Manchester City. You can ask Ola about football, sorry soccer, all day.

He plans to develop an app for fans of English Premier League where certain soccer tactics, performances and even trademarkable goals can be shared for rewards.
